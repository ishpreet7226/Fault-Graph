# Incident Summary — Unit 04 Condenser Fouling Cascade

During May 2024 Unit 04 experienced a recurring E3 high-pressure trip chain that was traced to a fouled condenser combined with a failed fan motor capacitor. Root cause analysis identified both reduced heat rejection and degraded fan motor components as contributing factors. Corrective actions included capacitor replacement, coil cleaning, and an updated PM schedule.
