# Refrigerant Leak Check

## Purpose
Use approved leak-detection procedures and document results

## Safety

- Follow site permit requirements

## Tools
- Multimeter
- Service manual
- LOTO kit

## Inspection
- Confirm conditions before starting work

## Repair
- Execute controlled repair steps and document results

## Verification

## Escalation
