# E1 PCB Control Error

**Mitsubishi E1** indicates a major malfunction or failure of the indoor unit's control board.

## Root Causes
1. **Power Surge**: A recent power surge may have damaged the board.
2. **Component Failure**: Internal failure of the PCB components.
3. **Loose Connection**: Main power or signal wiring may be loosely connected, causing erratic behavior.

## Safety Warnings
- ⚠️ Disconnect power before accessing the PCB.
- ⚠️ Check for proper grounding to prevent recurring PCB failures.

## Repair Steps
1. Disconnect all power to the system.
2. Inspect the indoor PCB for any signs of burning, electrical arcing, or bulging capacitors.
3. Verify that the incoming power supply voltage is correct and stable.
4. Ensure all connections on the board are secure.
5. If the power supply is stable and connections are good, replace the indoor PCB.
