# Evaporator

## Description
Heat absorption surface for cooling duty.

## Specifications
- Operating range: 40°F to 60°F leaving water
- Warranty: 24 months
- Typical service life: 8-12 years with preventive maintenance

## Failure Modes
- water-side fouling
- freeze-up

## Maintenance Schedule
- Quarterly water-side inspection
- Inspect connectors and fasteners each month
