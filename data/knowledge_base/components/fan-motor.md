# Fan Motor

## Description
Motor driving condenser or indoor fan assembly.

## Specifications
- Operating range: 460V/3Ph
- Warranty: 24 months
- Typical service life: 8-12 years with preventive maintenance

## Failure Modes
- overheating
- bearing wear

## Maintenance Schedule
- Monthly amperage and vibration
- Inspect connectors and fasteners each month
